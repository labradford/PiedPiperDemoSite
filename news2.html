Russ Hanneman Joins the PP Team

Russ Hanneman, the billionaire who "put radio on the internet" and creator of Tres Commas Tequila, has agreed to invest $5 million into Pied Piper.

The funding will help grow operations and staff as the company continues to develop and manage it's revolutionary data compression technology.
