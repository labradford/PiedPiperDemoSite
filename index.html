Pied Piper's Revolutionary Middle-Out Compression Technology

Pied Piper produces a revolutionary data compression engine based on CEO Richard Hendrick's middle-out file compression technology.

Our technology can take any time of files and compress them to an unprecedented level, making your data storage solutions easier.

We make your data storage problems smaller!

CEO Richard Hendricks

Richard Hendricks, Pied Piper CEO mostly fearless leader to our quirky but extremely talented team. Hendricks is the inventor of Pied Piper's signature middle-out compression technology method. Whatever it takes, he is willing to do to get things done. He is a brilliant coder, a fighter and always finds a way to move the company forward.

Board Member Erlich Bachman

Entrepreneur Erlich Bachman owns the technology development incubator that is home to Pied Piper. Bachman has the vision to lead Pied Piper to the future. He is confidant to CEO Richard Hendricks, offering his sage advice on how to manage and guide Pied Piper to its unlimited potential.

CFO Donald "Jared" Dunn

CFO Jared Dunn is head of Pied Piper's business development. Dunn manages processes and procedures. He provides the team with innovative methods to solve every day business issues. Dunn is a devoted employee who believes whole-heartedly in the potential of Pied Piper, and provides the glue that hold's the team together.

Co-CTO Bertram Gilfoyle

Co-CTO Bertram Gilfoyle is Pied Piper's systems architect. Gilfoyle hails from Canada but enjoys life in the heart of Silicon Valley. Gilfoyle is an online security expert and manages the security aspects of Pied Piper. Gilfoyle also designed and built the company's hardware systems.

Co-CTO Dinesh Chugai

Co-CTO Dinesh Chugai is our expert software programmer. Chugai is originally from Pakistan but is now a naturalized citizen of the United States. Chugtai works closely with co-CTO Bertram Gilfoyle but focuses on the software side of the business.

Engineer Carla Walton

Engineer Carla Walton recently joined the team after the big victory at the Tech Crunch Disrupt event. Walton brings a unique intelligence and a fresh perspective to the mostly-male team.
