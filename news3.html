Pied Piper Live Streams California Condor Nest

Pied Piper is proud to show off our data compression technology by live-streaming video of an unhatched condor egg!

The egg is in an unknown location to protect the fragile nature of the nest. Be sure to keep an eye out for the tell-tale signs of hatching, such as the subtle wiggling of the egg or small cracks in the shell.

Take notice of the smooth nature and amazing clarity in this video feed!
